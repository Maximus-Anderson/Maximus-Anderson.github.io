FSAE Tractive Battery Module

This project involved the design and manufacturing of a tractive system battery module for an FSAE electric vehicle. The module was optimized for high performance and safety, integrating advanced cell technology and thermal management systems.

Dynamometer Cart + Motor Testing

This project focused on designing a mobile dynamometer cart for testing electric motors. The system allowed for precise measurement of motor performance under various load conditions, aiding in the optimization of powertrain components.

1976 Honda CB750F Restoration

A full restoration of a 1976 Honda CB750F motorcycle, including engine rebuild, frame refinishing, and electrical system upgrades. The project preserved the classic design while enhancing performance and reliability.

2 Week Tube Chassis

A rapid-design project to create a lightweight tube chassis for a racing application, completed in two weeks. The chassis was engineered for structural integrity and ease of manufacturing.
